#77 — HIGH — /admin/layout.tsx auth-race retry
============================================================
Same race condition shape as Round 8 #53 (/teacher/layout) and Round
16 #72 (/student/layout). Single /api/auth/me call with no retry
means a transient 401 from Supabase auth-server eventual-consistency
falls through to platform_admin=false → real admin bounced to /
during the first ~300-800ms after sign-in.

This is HIGH not MEDIUM because the admin surface includes billing,
plan-proposal approvals, and school-onboarding flows — every minute
an admin spends re-trying logins is leverage lost on production
operations. Now uses the same 3-attempt × 300ms-backoff pattern as
the other two layouts.
