ROUND 11 — /teacher/generate visual overhaul
=============================================

Replaced the Round-9 "colored left border" treatment with proper modern-
app patterns:

  Hero: 40x40 gradient emerald-tile w/ Sparkles icon + 3xl bold h1 +
        readable subtitle + 5-step workflow dots
        (Class › Context › Test type › Source & mix › Generate).

  Step badges: numbered colored CIRCLES (1-4) — sky for setup-context
        (Steps 1-2), purple for the optional intent picker (Step 3),
        emerald for the primary work area (Step 4). Replaces the old
        "Step 1 · Title" text labels.

  Typography: section headings bumped text-sm font-semibold ->
        text-base font-bold; whitespace bumped mt-5 -> mt-6; mb-4
        inside each step header.

  Generate button: bigger padding (!px-6 !py-3), text-base font-
        semibold, md shadow on hover, "→" arrow suffix.

  "Will generate N questions" preflight: N is now 2xl bold emerald
        instead of inline strong — locks the eye on the commitment.

ROUND 12 + 13 — Build & Assign: 10 of 17 findings shipped
==========================================================

Build & Assign also got the hero-with-workflow-dots treatment
(Filter your bank › Select questions › Configure › Save & share).

Cross-validation:
  #55 HIGH (R12) Quiz name vs subject lexical mismatch warning.
        Catches "Math Chapter 5" named under subject="Science" via a
        family-token check across math / science / english / history /
        geography.
  #56 HIGH (R12) Time-limit × question-count cross-validation.
        Warns amber when <45 s/q (students rush), info-sky when
        >300 s/q (generous — flag, don't block).
  #57 MEDIUM (R13) Marking-scheme negative-marks × deep-Bloom mix.
        Warns when negative_marks is on AND >=60% of selected
        questions are Apply / Analyze / Evaluate / Create. Steers the
        teacher to a non-penalty preset.
  #59 MEDIUM (R13) bloomFilter × teaching-context warning.
        When the bank filter is set to a Bloom level the chosen exam
        context doesn't typically test (e.g. "remember" under CAT or
        "create" under NEET), surface a soft note. Mirrors the
        EXAM_DEFAULTS.supportedBloomLevels table from /teacher/generate.

Visual / UX:
  #61 HIGH (R12) Active filter pills above the bank with one-click
        clears + "Clear all".
  #62 MEDIUM (R12) Visible autosave indicator: green-dot +
        "Auto-saved at HH:MM" badge in the page header.
  #64 MEDIUM (R13) HTML5 native drag-drop reorder of selected
        questions. ⋮⋮ drag handle on each item, ring-2 ring-emerald
        on the drop target, opacity-60 on the dragged item. No library
        dependency.

Correctness:
  #66 LOW (R13) Code-uniqueness loop extended from 4 -> 8 retries,
        with a clear "Could not allocate a unique quiz code" error
        instead of relying on the downstream INSERT 23505.

Confirmation polish:
  #67 LOW (R12) mixedTopics confirm shows COUNT per topic
        (e.g. "Algebra (9 questions)") sorted by count desc — lets
        the teacher tell 9+1 (accidental) apart from 5+5 (intentional).
