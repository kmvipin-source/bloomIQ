FINDING #25 CLOSURE — next.config.ts ignoreBuildErrors = false PERMANENT
========================================================================
  The meta-cause of every critical bug in the audit. With all known TS
  errors closed in rounds 1-6, the build-time type-checker is now an
  invariant. Both gates are live:

    1. CI-time:    `tsc --noEmit -p tsconfig.check.json`  (strict:true)
    2. Build-time: `next build`  (ignoreBuildErrors:false)

  Any future PR introducing a TS error of the families this audit found —
  TS2304 (Cannot find name), TS2451 (block-scoped redeclaration), TS2339
  (missing property), TS2448/TS2454 (used-before-declared), TS2440
  (import conflicts with local), TS2322 (type assignment) — will fail
  both gates and cannot ship. Localized exceptions belong on a
  `// @ts-expect-error <reason>` line, never on a global flag.
